The matthorrigan.com favicon is an original mark: the site's asymmetric "leaf"
tile filled with the house pentad gradient, with cyan and orange leading and
violet, rose, and green woven through. It is generated from the brand tokens
(see brand/house-style.md and brand/pentad.py).

It replaced an earlier placeholder adapted from a Twitter Twemoji glyph (1f60c,
licensed CC-BY 4.0). That artwork is no longer used.
