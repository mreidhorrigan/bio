The matthorrigan.com favicon is the site's hero slime: the green gel avatar you
walk around the isometric world. It is a rounded dome on a flat base, filled with
a brand-green gel gradient (a light core, green body, darker rim), with a bright
sheen near the top-left and a dark ink outline. For the icon the slime gains two
friendly eyes with the pupils low, so it looks downward and peeks toward the page
below the browser tab.

The mark mirrors the in-world avatar (see defPaintAvatar in engine.js and the
theme avatarColors and avatarInk). The source vector is brand/favicon-slime.svg,
rasterised to each PNG and the multi-size .ico (see brand/house-style.md). The
home-screen icons sit on a soft cream backdrop, since iOS renders a transparent
icon on black.

It replaced an earlier brand "leaf" tile and, before that, a placeholder adapted
from a Twitter Twemoji glyph (1f60c, licensed CC-BY 4.0). Neither is used now.
